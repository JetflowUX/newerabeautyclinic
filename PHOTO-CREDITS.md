## These are stock photos, not the clinic

They illustrate the kind of treatments offered. They are **not** photographs of New Era Beauty
Clinic, its team, or its clients' results, and the alt text is written accordingly. Before any
image is captioned as the clinic's own work or as a client result, replace it with the clinic's
own photography — and get written client consent for any before/after shots. Presenting stock or
generated imagery as real results would breach the ASA/CAP rules on cosmetic advertising.
